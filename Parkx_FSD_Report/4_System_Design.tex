\chapter{SYSTEM DESIGN }

\section{Project Design}
System design involves planning the structure, components, data flows, and interfaces of an application to fulfill a set of specific requirements organically. Strong system design ensures the product has room to grow, avoids architectural bottlenecks, and delivers a robust, responsive feedback loop to the driver, owner, or system admin.

In ParkX, the system architecture operates via an MVC structure specifically customized to favor transaction integrity holding exact parking times.

\subsection{User Interface (UI) Design}
The UI Aesthetics of ParkX strictly follow an "Antigravity" design blueprint. This relies on semi-transparent glassmorphic overlays, vibrant floating elements, and sharp border-radius implementations. Unlike standard dashboard panels, the interface encourages visually appealing micro-interactions to signify digital modernity.

\subsection{Database Design}
The ParkX database leverages normalized relational models using PostgreSQL. Crucial tables include:

\subsubsection{Core Application Tables}

\paragraph{Table: \texttt{users}}
Stores authentication and role credentials for the system.
\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{3cm}|p{2cm}|p{6cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Column Name} & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Key} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \hline
    \endhead
    id & BigInt & PK & Unique identifier for the user \\
    \hline
    name & Varchar(255) & - & Full name of the user \\
    \hline
    email & Varchar(255) & Unique & Login email address \\
    \hline
    role & Enum & - & Defined as 'student', 'admin', or 'owner' \\
    \hline
    password & Varchar(255) & - & Hash of the user's password \\
    \hline
\end{longtable}

\paragraph{Table: \texttt{vehicle\_categories}}
Defines universally available vehicle types and pricing matrices.
\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{3cm}|p{2cm}|p{6cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Column Name} & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Key} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \hline
    \endhead
    id & BigInt & PK & Unique identifier for the category \\
    \hline
    name & Varchar(255) & - & E.g., 'Two Wheeler', 'Four Wheeler' \\
    \hline
    base\_charge & Decimal(8,2) & - & The flat entry fee for this vehicle type \\
    \hline
    hourly\_rate & Decimal(8,2) & - & The recurring hourly cost \\
    \hline
\end{longtable}

\subsubsection{Physical Infrastructure Tables}

\paragraph{Table: \texttt{parking\_spaces}}
Represents a physical parking venue or lot managed by an owner.
\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{3cm}|p{2cm}|p{6cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Column Name} & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Key} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \hline
    \endhead
    id & BigInt & PK & Unique identifier for the parking space \\
    \hline
    owner\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{users} table (Role: Owner) \\
    \hline
    name & Varchar(255) & - & Name of the parking venue \\
    \hline
    location\_address & Text & - & Physical address of the venue \\
    \hline
\end{longtable}

\paragraph{Table: \texttt{parking\_space\_owners}}
Stores the extended profile details of approved space owners.
\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{3cm}|p{2cm}|p{6cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Column Name} & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Key} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \hline
    \endhead
    id & BigInt & PK & Unique identifier for the owner profile \\
    \hline
    user\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{users} table (Role: Owner) \\
    \hline
    contact & Varchar(255) & - & Contact phone number \\
    \hline
    address & Varchar(255) & - & Personal residential or office address \\
    \hline
    status & Enum & - & Defined as 'active' or 'inactive' \\
    \hline
\end{longtable}

\paragraph{Table: \texttt{parking\_slots}}
Represents a distinct individual parking slot within a broader parking space.
\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{3cm}|p{2cm}|p{6cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Column Name} & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Key} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \hline
    \endhead
    id & BigInt & PK & Unique identifier for the slot \\
    \hline
    space\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{parking\_spaces} table \\
    \hline
    category\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{vehicle\_categories} table \\
    \hline
    slot\_identifier & Varchar(50) & - & E.g., 'A1', 'B4' \\
    \hline
    is\_active & Boolean & - & Determines if the slot is currently bookable \\
    \hline
\end{longtable}

\subsubsection{Transaction \& Presence Tables}

\paragraph{Table: \texttt{vehicles}}
Tracks the physical presence and financial metrics of cars currently inside the venue.
\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{3cm}|p{2cm}|p{6cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Column Name} & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Key} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \hline
    \endhead
    id & BigInt & PK & Unique identifier for the vehicle record \\
    \hline
    parking\_space\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{parking\_spaces} table \\
    \hline
    category\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{vehicle\_categories} table \\
    \hline
    user\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{users} table (Nullable) \\
    \hline
    vehicle\_number & Varchar(255) & - & The license plate number \\
    \hline
    entry\_time & Timestamp & - & Time the vehicle entered the venue \\
    \hline
    exit\_time & Timestamp & - & Time the vehicle exited (Nullable) \\
    \hline
    duration & Integer & - & Total parked duration in minutes \\
    \hline
    charge & Decimal(10,2) & - & Calculated base cost \\
    \hline
    penalty & Decimal(10,2) & - & Late exit penalties \\
    \hline
    status & Enum & - & Defined as 'parked' or 'exited' \\
    \hline
\end{longtable}

\paragraph{Table: \texttt{bookings}}
Connects a User to a specific Slot for a specific time and records transactional data.
\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{3cm}|p{2cm}|p{6cm}|}
    \hline
    \textbf{Column Name} & \textbf{Data Type} & \textbf{Key} & \textbf{Description} \\
    \hline
    \endhead
    id & BigInt & PK & Unique identifier for the booking \\
    \hline
    user\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{users} table (Consumer) \\
    \hline
    slot\_id & BigInt & FK & Links to \texttt{parking\_slots} table \\
    \hline
    reservation\_time & Timestamp & - & Exact time range of the booking slot \\
    \hline
    status & Enum & - & E.g., 'Pending', 'Confirmed', 'Completed' \\
    \hline
    vehicle\_video & Varchar(255) & - & Path to 10MB condition proof video upload \\
    \hline
\end{longtable}
